# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Minecraft Forge players operating a compact bio-industrial production chain. They need to understand machine state quickly, move items reliably, and control processing without memorizing hidden right-click sequences.

## Product Purpose

Cyber-Cultivator: Bio-Synthesis turns genetic breeding and serum production into a readable, deterministic machine workflow. The machine interfaces should make inputs, progress, outputs, automation, and exceptional states visible while preserving Minecraft inventory conventions.

## Brand Personality

Industrial, clinical, precise. The interface should feel like durable laboratory hardware rather than a futuristic hologram or a generic web dashboard.

## Anti-references

Avoid glossy gradients, oversized decoration, excessive neon, ambiguous icon-only controls, and layouts that hide vanilla inventory behavior. Do not imitate modern SaaS cards or use color as the only status signal.

## Design Principles

1. Show the complete input-to-output path at a glance.
2. Reuse vanilla slot, tooltip, and click behavior wherever possible.
3. Reserve accent colors for machine state and active material channels.
4. Keep all four machines visually related while giving each a distinct process diagram.
5. Make automation modes and blocked states explicit and reversible.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Every state uses shape, text, or fill level in addition to color. Controls provide translated tooltips, disabled feedback, and sufficient contrast at every Minecraft GUI scale. Motion is limited to process indicators and never required to understand state.
